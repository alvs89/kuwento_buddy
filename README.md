🌟 **KuwentoBuddy: Your Interactive Reading Companion!** 📖✨

**KuwentoBuddy** is a mobile application designed to help users improve their reading comprehension skills through an interactive and engaging experience. Using a unique **"Read-Think-Continue"** method, the app ensures that readers don't just recognize words but truly understand the story by pausing at key moments to check for comprehension.

---

### ✨ **Key Features** 🌟

* 
**Interactive Story Engine (Read-Think-Continue)** 📖⏯️: Stories are divided into parts where the app automatically pauses at critical plot points. Users must answer comprehension questions before they can continue reading the next segment.


* 
**Supportive Buddy Companion** 🤖🤝: A friendly digital buddy stays on screen to guide the user. It provides instant feedback, such as happy animations for correct answers and helpful hints when a user is stuck.


* 
**Leveled Content Library** 📚🏘️: Stories are organized into user-friendly categories and difficulty levels, such as **Beginner**, **Intermediate**, and **Advanced**. It includes featured narratives like "The Magical Forest" and local "Filipino Tales".


* 
**Sequence the Story Activity** 🧩🔄: After finishing a story, users can practice narrative structure through a "Put It in Order" game where they arrange key events chronologically.


* 
**My Progress Dashboard** 📊📈: Users can track their reading journey with stats like "Stories Read," "Comprehension %," and "Correct Answers" to stay motivated.


* 
**Spotify-Inspired UI/UX** 🎨🎶: The app features a clean, modern design with horizontal library scrolling and "Album Art" style story covers for a premium feel.
